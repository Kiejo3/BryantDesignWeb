Jacob Bryant

Design, Motion & Creative

Hello, I am a passionate creative based in London and Norwich — dedicated to my projects and always hoping to learn. From time to time I create artwork, which allows me to stay creative and have a good knowledge of coding and computers, having designed and developed this website from scratch. I try to stay active as much as I can and I enjoy running, swimming and bouldering.

My creative journey began at age 10 with an inital interest in doodles and drawings, which evolved into creating comics by 13. I expanded my hobbies into coding when I was 14, which quickly evolved into game design when I was 15. I developed my appreciation for fine arts untill I was 18, before going to university to study Graphic Communication, where I am now.

My years of experimenting with hobbies has allowed me to stretch my way of thinking and push the boundaries of creative projects. Being creative is what I am passionate about, and I love that I can continue to push creativity through new mediums in my design work. I am a well-rounded designer, but I specialise in motion and ideas. I can't wait to meet you!
