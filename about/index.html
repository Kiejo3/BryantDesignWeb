Who is Jacob Bryant?

Designer & Creative

Hi, I’m Jacob, a 21 year old designer in London and Norwich. I am passionate, energetic and creative, dedicated to my projects and always hoping to learn. From time to time I still create art pieces and still have a good knowledge of coding and computers, having designed and developed this website from scratch. I try to stay active as much as I can and I enjoy running, swimming and bouldering.

My creative journey began at age 10 with an inital interest in doodles and drawings, which evolved into creating comics by 13. I expanded my hobbies into coding when I was 14, which quickly evolved into game design when I was 15. I developed my appreciation for fine arts untill I was 18, before exploring the world of graphic design, which is the field I am in now.
