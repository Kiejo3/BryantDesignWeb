About me

Who is Jacob Bryant?

How would I describe myself?

A buoyant, happy and bubbly personality. Observant, creative and detail oriented in all things design. An adaptable, organised and strong communicator in teams and independently.

What parts of design do I like?

I like to explore motion, and combined with my interest in visual identities I enjoy creating animated positive dynamic and brand identities My coding skills and interest in niche software and websites translates to an interest in UI/UX design. I am able to develop digital solutions (e.g. websites) Finally, my interest in 3D design allows me to create full worlds for brands and explore a third dimension in my design work.
