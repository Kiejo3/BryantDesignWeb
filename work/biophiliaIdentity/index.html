Biophilia.

Definition: A hypothetical human tendency to interact or be closely associated with other forms of life in nature. A desire or tendency to commune with nature. This exhibition displayed the beauty and movement of the growth of plants and exaggerate the calm they can help us to feel

Date

Febuary 2024

Tags

Event Identity

This project explored the creation of a full identity for an exhibition called Biophillia, which explored the connection between plants and people and the human need for nature in their daily lives. Creating a full identity allowed me to explore a response across multiple touchpoints, which encouraged the design to continue growing through more applications.

Being a longer project, it allowed me to learn about the process and lifecycle of a bigger project. Finally, it allowed me to continue developing new technologies and abstract methods of communication.
