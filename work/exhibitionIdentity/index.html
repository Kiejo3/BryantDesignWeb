Exhibition Identity

Creating a thematically correct identity for an exhibition

Project outcomes

•     1x promotional animation

•     2x digital posters

•     10x physical poster set

•     2x billboards

•     1x exhibition leaflet

•     1x exhibition content design

•     5x seed packet series

•     10x physical poster set

My response

This project explored the creation of a full identity for an exhibition called Biophillia, which explored the connection between plants and people and the human need for nature in their daily lives. It focused on exploring the positive impacts that plants have on mental health. 

Creating a full identity allowed me to explore a response across multiple touchpoints, which encouraged the design to continue growing through more applications. Being a longer project, I learned about the process and lifecycle of a bigger project. Finally, it allowed me to continue developing new technologies and abstract methods of communication.
