Motion Projects

Learning how to animate simple words and using after effects for the first time, has opened my doors to the world of motion and how it can improve projects by helping an audience better understand he design idea, or by making it more engaging.

Title Sequence

This intro sequence is for a made-up show called "The Hidden Dances of the World." I took inspiration from various dances from various cultures and used the circle to represent this movement. I had this circle interact with various scenes to create interaction and flow from one scene to the next.
