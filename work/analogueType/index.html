Concept Project

Analogue Type.

Creating a set of magazine spreads in the style of killing eve.

Magazine Design

Creative

Requirements

3x Magazine spreads

1x Analogue type

The hero of the design, the analogue type, is created out of ribbon to best express the style of the show, Killing Eve.

Ribbons create a twisting, mesmerising path, and the way it curves and flows through a scene is quite seductive.

Visually, the colour red and ribbons are already used as key themes within the show.

The layout guides the reader around the page using the ribbon and white space. By using lots of blank space around the design the text is the main focus of the page, and brings the most attention to the analogue type.
